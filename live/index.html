Points Criteria:

  • Houses earn points based on their rank in each game.
  • Points per position:
    • 1st place: 10 points
    • 2nd place: 7 points
    • 3rd place: 5 points
  • Add up points from all games to get the total score.
  • Example:
    • A house got:
      • 1st place in 10 games: 10 × 10 = 100 points
      • 2nd place in 5 games: 5 × 7 = 35 points
      • 3rd place in 2 games: 2 × 5 = 10 points
    • Total points: 100 + 35 + 10 = 145 points.
  • The Grand Total scores of houses include points from all school activities held earlier during the session, such as soft board decoration, debates etc.
